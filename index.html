About Me

I was born in Southern California, but I spent time living in Washington, Texas, New Jersey, Hawaii and Utah before I left for College. I studied economics... and immediately went into hospitality.

I like to travel, Last year I traveled to Machu Picchu, Chicago and spent some time in Paris. I mostly just ate macaroons and baguettes until I passed out. Right now i'm planning a trip out to Japan... or the mediterranean for this winter. I've come to realize that people in hospitality work too hard, and I really like expensive hobbies... so I made the leap to programming.

If I'm being brutally honest, I probably got into programming primarily so that I can program my own Magic Mirror (raspberry pi project...) but the great career options is a nice bonus.
